About

I love small babies and tiny children, with my babysitting age range being anywhere from 1 week to 102 years old. The feelings I get when I realize I have absolute control over their childish, little lives empowers me to be a better person for tomorrow.

I have a strict training regimen to potty-train small children, and a stricter one to maintain that as they age. In addition to that, I have a personal goal to instill ethics and good moral values onto each little child that comes to me.

Many of the children I've babysat have gone on to lead famous, influential lives, such as Presidents, Felons, Actors, Actresses, and a really weird clown.
